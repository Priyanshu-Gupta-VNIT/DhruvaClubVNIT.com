What We Did During Pandemic Time?

Pandemic time was tough for us. But we took this challenge as an opportunity.
We continuously meet with the students through zoom meets. We invited guest speakers who are working in MNC's and were former part of Dhruva Club.
We also do meditation sessions daily for 15 minutes.

We wish students HAPPY BIRTHDAY in unique way by sending interesting books to them. They can get useful learnings from these books and can apply them in their life. Example- OPEN-EYED MEDITATION. Also we teach lessons from LEADERSHIP LEARNINGS FROM BHAGVAD GITA during meets.
